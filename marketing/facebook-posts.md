### Post (launch day)

I built a Mac app for testing film sim recipes on your actual camera.

It's basically a way to build recipes from scratch, one setting at a time, seeing real renders instead of guessing.

### Post (shorter, SOOC-focused angle)

Built a Mac tool for dialing in film sim recipes using your camera's actual processor.

The renders are identical to what you'd get shooting JPEG — same processor, same pipeline. Useful for building SOOC recipes when you want to see exactly what each setting does.
