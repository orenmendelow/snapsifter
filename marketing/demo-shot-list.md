## Production Notes

- **Speed ramping:** Shots 5 and 6 need time compression for the simulation renders (real-time is 8-10s per variant). Use jump cuts or 4x speed with motion blur during waiting, return to 1x when results appear.
- **Cursor visibility:** Use a slightly larger cursor or add a subtle highlight/trail so actions are trackable at 1080p.
- **Key presses:** Consider showing a small key indicator overlay (bottom-right, transparent) during Shot 3 so viewers see which key was pressed. Optional — test without first.
- **Audio:** Music starts at 0:02 (under the title), low volume, peaks slightly during Shot 5 simulation sequence, fades under end card.
- **Social crop (1080x1080):** Center-crop the screen recording. May need to re-record with a narrower window or zoom into the active area per shot.
- **Thumbnail:** Frame from Shot 5 (full variant grid, 10 film sims visible) or Shot 6 (SBS with zoomed detail).
